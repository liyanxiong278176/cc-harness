The hero section above is exactly 100vh tall. The ScrollTrigger watches the page scroll position and drives a GSAP tween that translates the headline upward while fading its opacity to zero.

Because the tween uses scrub: true, the animation is bound directly to scroll position — moving the timeline forward when you scroll down and backward when you scroll up.

Keep scrolling to see more placeholder content. The hero animation is fully reversible: scroll back up and the title glides back into place.
